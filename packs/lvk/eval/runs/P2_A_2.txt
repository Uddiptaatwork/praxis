## GW190521 — Source-Frame Component Masses

From the GWTC-2 catalog (Abbott et al. 2020, ApJL 900, L13), using the preferred NRSur7dq4 waveform model:

| Parameter | Median | 90% Credible Interval |
|---|---|---|
| Primary mass *m*₁ | 85 M☉ | +21 / −14 M☉ |
| Secondary mass *m*₂ | 66 M☉ | +17 / −18 M☉ |

So:

- **m₁ = 85⁺²¹₋₁₄ M☉**
- **m₂ = 66⁺¹⁷₋₁₈ M☉**

The primary mass falls squarely in the pair-instability supernova gap (~60–130 M☉), making GW190521 exceptional. The final remnant mass is ~142 M☉ (the first unambiguous intermediate-mass black hole from a merger).
