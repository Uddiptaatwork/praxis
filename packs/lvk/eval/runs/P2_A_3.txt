**GW190521 — Source-frame component masses (90% credible intervals)**

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Primary mass $m_1$ | $85.4^{+21.0}_{-14.2}\ M_\odot$ |
| Secondary mass $m_2$ | $66.0^{+17.0}_{-18.3}\ M_\odot$ |

Both components fall in the pair-instability supernova mass gap (~65–135 $M_\odot$), making GW190521 notable as the first BBH merger with both components in that gap. The remnant (~142 $M_\odot$) is the first direct observation of an intermediate-mass black hole.
