For **GW150914**, the two source-frame component masses (black hole primary and secondary) with **90% credible intervals** are:

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Primary mass m₁ | **35.6 ⁺⁴·⁸₋₃.₁ M☉** |
| Secondary mass m₂ | **30.6 ⁺³·³₋₄.₄ M☉** |

These are from the LVC parameter estimation results (Abbott et al. 2016, PRL 116, 061102), and are reproduced on the GWOSC event page.
